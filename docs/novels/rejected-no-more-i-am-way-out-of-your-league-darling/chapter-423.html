Once, she had been a bright little girl, clinging to his leg and begging for his affection.

But now, standing before him, was something unrecognizable, a ghostly figure-cold, detached,and utterly different.

Though Daniela was keenly aware of the storm of thoughts raging in Caiden's mind, she remained indifferent.

She asked, "Have you figured out how to answer my question?"

Caiden bit his lower lip hard, trying to control his reaction.

Daniela laughed, her smile as charming as ever. The moon peeked through the clouds, lighting up her already glowing face, and giving her a fairy-like appearance.

However, her actions were swift and unhesitating.With a subtle movement, she pressed, and Caiden's agonized scream echoed through the silence.

Daniela said casually, "It seems like the knife went right through your thigh."

She bent over and vomited.

It was unlike anything she'd ever seen-a brutal,bloody revenge that seemed to stretch the limits of human cruelty. Fear clutched at her heart, but something darker, something inexplicably thriling,stirred deep within.

Wiping her mouth with firm resolve, Nina grasped the door handle and stepped back into the chaos.

Caiden had already passed out.

Daniela brought a bucket of water, and Caiden stirred as though he had narrowly escaped death.

Daniela watched him, smiling. "Hi. We meet again."

Before he could, Daniela's laugh broke the silence. "Don't bother. I've got a box of adrenaline right here.Like | said, I won't let you die."

With that, she yanked the knife from Caiden's thigh and drove it back in.

His screams filled the air, but Daniela didn't flinch.She took a seat on the nearby sofa, her posture relaxed. "Come on, then. Tell me. What were the conditions for this intermediary to spill my mother's secrets and whereabouts?"

Together with Elyse, the hypnotist who reached out to her, and that intermediary, they had carefully laid out a deadly plot.
