Chapter 121

Alexander was seething with fury, feeling like he would explode at any moment.

Upon his arrival at the bar, he found Keith swaying slightly, clearly drunk and unsteady.

When Alexander shared his grievances, Keith's eyes widened in astonishment, realizing the truth in his friend's words.

Breaking it down for him, Keith remarked thoughtfully, "Tell me if I'm off base here, Alexander.Wasn't it Daniela who was always following you around? She used to hang on your every word while you barely gave her the time of day. Now,she's wealthy, and suddenly you're showing interest. No wonder she questions your motives. Daniela makes a good point,you know."

Alexander clenched his jaw, his expression growing stormy as he gulped down his drink forcefully. "So you're suggesting I play hard to get?"

Memories of Cedric's tender ways with Daniela flashed through his mind. "If I delay too long, she might settle down and start a family with Cedric!"

"Just keep showing her kindness," Keith advised, his words slurring as the alcohol took over. "People have hearts; they're not just emotionless stones. Be patient with her, whether it's for a year, two years,or even a decade. Alexander, your pride is getting in the way. You act as if everyone should adore you! Daniela has changed; what makes you think she'll return to you? You need to change your approach.Constantly expecting her to appease you will only drive her away. As a man, don't let pride dictate your actions,especially since she's your ex-wife. Why fuss about who's right?"

With a hearty slap on the back, Keith exclaimed,"What a foolish man you are!"

And with that,Keith crumpled to the floor in a heap.

Alexander watched, making no move to assist him. He sat in his chair, ensnared by his own thoughts for what felt like an eternity.

Eventually, his voice broke the silence, soft and uncertain. "Is that really how it is? One year... two years... a decade? If I chase after her, will Daniela ever return to me?"

Daniela, ever the efficient worker, had completed her assignments the previous evening. Her secretary popped in with a reminder, bubbling with enthusiasm. "Daniela, don't forget about the banquet tonight."

Daniela's expression shadowed with a touch of melancholy.

The secretary departed with an uncontainable grin.Throughout her tenure, she had never encountered a CEO as genuinely charming as Daniela.

"What's got you in such a good mood? You look exceptionally cheerful."

Meanwhile, Alexander stepped out of the restroom.He caught the sound of Daniela's laughter.

It was her happiness that echoed in her laughter-happiness sparked by his message.

Her laughter rang melodious, stirringa wave of elation in Alexander's heart.

He now believed Keith's advice. If he persisted in showing genuine kindness to Daniela, she would undoubtedly feel his sincerity.

It was only a matter of time before everything Daniela had became his.

He wished, with all his heart, that Daniela could go back to being the person she once was.
