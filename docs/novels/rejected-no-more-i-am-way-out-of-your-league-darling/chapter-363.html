Chapter 359

Daniela flashed a smirk and said, "You claim to have no money, yet Joyce has plenty."

Katrina's expression tightened. "Joyce avoids me. She only sends me twenty thousand dollars a month."

Replicating Katrina's earlier tone with a playful smile, Daniela said, "There's a banquet tonight where Joyce will be present. I'll bring you along. As her mother, she won't be able to dismiss you."

Katrina realized Daniela was cleverly using her own logic to corner her. Though hesitant, she saw no alternative.

Daniela instantly read Katrina's reluctance. "You've always been there for Joyce, far more than Caiden ever was for me. It's only fair that Joyce supports you."

Katrina found no fault in that logic and remained silent.

Lillian then took a disheartened Katrina to change into the evening attire for the event.

As they prepared to depart, Katrina caught sight of Nina by the doorway.

Her reaction was immediate: pupils dilating and complexion paling as a shiver ran through her.

Instinctively, she sidestepped and hurried out of Daniela's office.

Nina, lingering at the door,reflected on her observations.

Initially, she had underestimated Daniela, attributing her success to mere luck.

However, witnessing Daniela command a room during meetings changed her perspective.

Daniela's dominance and the respect she commanded were undeniable.

Today, as she watched Daniela handle Katrina with such deft authority and strategic conversation, Nina was convinced of Daniela's complexity. Quietly, Nina bowed her head and texted Elyse:

"Daniela will be at the banquet tonight, wrapping up around eleven. I'll keep Cedric busy, Handle Daniela on your end!"

After sending the text, Nina shifted her focus to Lillian.

Lillian appeared experienced in handling difficult situations. Nina knew she had to find a way to prevent Lillian from attending the banquet, isolating Daniela for the evening.

After a brief pause, Nina swung open the door to Daniela's office, wearing a bright smile and adopting an overly sweet tone. "Daniela, it seems like I'm not on your radar at all."

"Why?" Daniela responded with a light chuckle.

"Are you attending the banquet tonight? You haven't considered inviting me to join you for some networking.Are you only taking Lillian? We are family, after all."

Daniela arched an eyebrow. "Is that so?"

"Yes, we are all part of the Dury family. We should stick together and lift each other up. Please, Daniela, I'd love to go to the banquet with you tonight. Leave Lillian behind."

Daniela's smile was gentle, her voice teasing, "You're quite demanding, aren't you? Why can't Lillian come?"

Nina made a face, resembling a sulking child. "I just had a small argument with Lillian. I need some space.Daniela, please, just take me."

Daniela gave a nod."Okay."

Nina's face lit up with delight. She quickly glanced at Cedric, who was preoccupied with his phone, and her heart skipped with excitement.

"Daniela, may I stop by Elite Lux's high-end designer section to pick a dress?"

Daniela gave her permission with a nod, "Of course, go pick something nice."

Nina's eyes sparkled. "Thanks so much, Daniela!" As Nina exited the office, she strutted confidently, assured by her victory

Tonight, she believed, Cedric would be completely captivated by her.
