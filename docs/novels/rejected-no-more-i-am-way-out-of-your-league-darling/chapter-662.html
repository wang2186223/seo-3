Chapter 658

Daniela took a bite of the breakfast, chewing without much thought.

Caiden began to eat the rest andasked Cedric, "This is amazing! Where'd you buy it?"

Cedric's gaze followed Daniela as she disappeared up the stairs, laptop in hand.

He turned to Caiden, eyebrows raised. "What was that about earlier? The wealthiest family?"

Caiden choked mid-bite, the food lodged uncomfortably in his throat. After coughing for a while,he said, "I said nothing!"

Cedric, sensing something off, narrowed his eyes."This got anything to do with me."

Caiden froze, jaw slack in shock.

He attempted a quick getaway, but Cedric's voice,laced with quiet menace, stopped him cold. "If my wife can take someone down, so can l."

Caiden looked ready to weep. "Come on, have mercy!You both treat me like the weakest link and keep ganging up on me!"

And with that, he took off like a startled rabbit.

Cedric squinted, lost in thought, his gaze lingering on the animated show Daniela had left playing

After a moment, his attention drifted to Jack,happily lost in his toys on the floor.

When Daniela returned downstairs, she found Cedric in the kitchen, side by side with Ruth, cooking.She had never met a man so effortlessly domestic.

He wasn't the type to frequent bars, brag about his achievements, or waste hours socializing.

Instead, he found contentment in quiet routines cooking up meals, washing fruit for her, tending to the garden.Simple things, yet strangely reassuring.

"Cedric is truly remarkable," Caiden remarked. Ever since discovering Cedric was Hamilton's eldest son,he had grown even fonder of Cedric. "He moves seamlessly between the boardroom and the kitchen,yet never asks anything of you. If your mother were still here, she would be proud."

It had been years since Caiden had willingly brought up Brylee.

Daniela tilted her head,studying him curiously. Caiden went on, "A real man shoulders responsibility so his wife has someone to lean on. Cedric has that,and he treasures you. That's all that really matters."

Daniela leaned back into the plush sofa, saying nothing. Her gaze flickered to the television, now tuned to the same channel that had just aired Hamilton's interview.

Her eyes clouded over, lingering in deep thought before drifting toward Cedric.

Elite Lux's grand fashion show wrapped up abruptly,catching many off guard.

Most assumed the show had ended because Elite Lux had already hit Daniela's ambitious sales targets.

But in her office, with a steely gaze and fingers tapping rhythmically on her desk, Daniela was plotting something far bigger-relocating Elite Lux's Olisvine branch to Oiscoll.

The announcement sent shockwaves through the entire industry.

Questions flooded in, and among them, Caiden,grinning, asked, "If you're moving, what happens to this villa?"

Daniela didn't even glance up. "Cedric and I went through our share of struggles after marriage. Consider this villa my way of making it up to him."

Caiden's expression soured. "And your other properties in Olisvine?"

Daniela's lips parted. "Those too..."

Before she could complete her sentence, Carol stepped in, her voice low as she leaned toward Daniela. "Daniela, urgent news-Hamilton is set to name one of his five children as his heir. They've been assigned a city to develop."

Daniela's eyes sharpened as she turned to Carol."Which city?"

Carol replied, "Here."

Elite Lux had been set to relocate, but now, that plan was suddenly in limbo.

The news sent the public into a haze of uncertainty.

Caiden sat off to the side, unusually silent, emotions buried beneath a carefully composed expression.

Earlier,he had asked Daniela what she would leave him once she left the country. She hadn't given a direct answer, but her silence had spoken volumes.

She had no sentiment for their father-daughter relationship, nor any intention of leaving him a single asset. To Daniela, he had never been a father-only a tool in her relentless pursuit of her mother's killer.

Once, Caiden had foolishly believed that time might soften Daniela's heart, that she might come to care for him. But he had miscalculated.

Her heart was as unyielding as ever-frozen,unreachable.

With that realization settling in, Caiden knew it was time to stop waiting for scraps and start securing his own future.
