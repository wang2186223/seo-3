Lillian's eyes widened. "Who's throwing around that kind of money?"

Kidnapping wasn't murder. This was about claiming Cedric.

Lillian gestured upstairs. "Does he know?"

Daniela didn't even glance up. "It's minor. No need to inform him."

Lillian smirked knowingly. "Someone spent eight hundred million just to snatch Cedric. That's some serious devotion. Looks like you have competition!"

Daniela remained indifferent. "Not many can afford that kind of money. I'll find out who."

A sum like that wouldn't go unnoticed in the banking system. Daniela reached for a laptop and started typing.

From the corner of her eye, she noticed someone descending the stairs.

Cedric stood on the staircase, gazing at her with feigned sadness. "Your thirty minutes are up."

Daniela sighed, handing the laptop to Ryan. "You take over the search."

Lillian folded her arms, mildly irritated at how Daniela indulged Cedric. She remarked pointedly, "Daniela's busy. Cedric, maybe be a little considerate?"
