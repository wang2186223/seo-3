Chapter 112

Daniela wasn't moved at all, and she even reprimanded Alexander. This sparked a flare of irritation within him.

He considered himself a sought-after man, not one to be dismissed lightly.

Daniela's demeanor, suggesting he was somehow desperate for her attention, was intolerable to him.

Yet, when she drove away from the parking lot, he couldn't help but trail behind her.

With Daniela at the helm of a billion-dollar project,he knew staying close was his only option-if he didn't,someone else surely would.

This realization was a blow to his ego. Alexander had never felt so diminished.

At the construction site, Daniela dove into her tasks with unwavering dedication. Alexander, donning a safety helmet, remained by her side throughout the day.

What caught him off guard was her expertise-she wasn't only giving orders but knew the intricacies of architecture, engineering, and market trends by heart.

In several respects, her expertise eclipsed his own,despite his ten years in the industry.

This revelation cast Daniela in an entirely different light.

He observed her,her delicate features accentuated by the helmet, exuding a sharp concentration. She even seemed to grasp the surveyor's techniques intuitively.

During the lunch break, Alexander walked over to her with a meal he had brought along.

But Daniela, leaning into the brisk wind, was engrossed in a phone conversation.

Alexander's ears perked as he overheard snippets of her phone conversation. "Dreamscape Research Institute must quicken its steps. Our heart restoration technology already leads the world, but I need sharper,more intricate data. Let's hold off on notifying marketing-more thorough evaluations are essential before we push forward with experimental surgical initiatives."

She delivered each word with a resolute and commanding air.

On the other end of the line, someone responded submissively, "Understood."

Alexander stood motionless, his lunchbox dangling in his grasp, as a surge of realization overwhelmed him.

Dreamscape was the titan of tech-medical research,a behemoth reigning over the medical sector for decades, boasting over 50, 000 medical patents globally.

The implication was clear-Dreamscape's workforce casually generated billions annually through their patents alone.

The researchers at Dreamscape were an elusive league,comprising the world's top ten intellects. They shunned the public eye, yet their rare appearances heralded monumental advancements.

The Bennett family had once ventured into the medical sector, but it proved too intricate and profound for them; their attempts amounted to nothing more than modest endeavors.

Lately, whispers had circulated throughout the global medical circles that Dreamscape had pioneered a groundbreaking technology for repairing heart damage. This innovation promised a definitive remedy for cardiac ailments, potentially eradicating congenital heart defects in patients.

Their claim was almost unbelievable-a single pill could restore heart function and breathe life into those who thought all hope was lost.

News of the pill spread like wildfire, leaving the entire planet buzzing with anticipation.

Questions swirled like a storm. Who were the unseen geniuses driving this monumental force? What marvels were unfolding within their brilliant minds?

Stunned into silence, Alexander couldn't tear his gaze away from Daniela's graceful frame.

It dawned on him that the very person he had been searching for had been right before his eyes the entire time.

The thought made him sick-he had actuallychoose Joyce over Daniela, like a fool chasing scraps.

It was as if Alexander's chest had been ripped open,his heart left bleeding.

Daniela pivoted, her eyes narrowing on Alexander,who stood there, his face ashen and beads of sweat forming despite the sweltering heat.

"What are you doing here?" she inquired,her voice tinged with suspicion.

Alexander's response lingered in the air, his eyes distant and unfocused. Suddenly,he erupted into a fit of laughter-loud and wild,bordering on the edge of madness.

With an intense glare, she demanded, "What's so funny?"

His laughter erupted even louder, tears now cascading freely down his flushed face.

Lillian stepped in, positioning herself between them."He's completely lost his mind, Daniela. Avoid him at all costs-it's for your safety!"

Undeterred,Daniela sidestepped him, returning to her tasks with a dismissive shake of her head.

Alexander's laughter was still ringing through the air when his phone vibrated. It was a call from Richard.

Initially, Richard was puzzled by Alexander's unrestrained laughter, but he quickly grasped the situation.

"Has Daniela finally agreed to relinquish the construction rights?" he probed,his voice laced with impatience.

"No, Dad," Alexander responded, a hint of sardonic humor coloring his tone. "Daniela's expertise is such that she can manage those projects with her eyes closed." Richard's response was a dismissive scoff. "Bullshit! A partnership with us is a no-brainer. Why the fuck would she refuse?"

The laughter that escaped Alexander then was tinged with cynicism.

′′Apartnership? That's the most absurd thing l've heard all year! Dad, do you truly understand who Daniela is?" he queried.

Richard's irritation was palpable as he retorted sharply,"What about her? She's just Daniela. So what if she's the CEO of Elite Lux? Has Elite Lux become so prosperous that it's now invincible?"

In that moment, Alexander's laughter came to an abrupt halt.

Straightening his posture, he fixed his eyes on Daniela.The evening gloom cast her into a silhouette, barely discernible in the darkening horizon.

Turning back his attention to Richard, he said into the phone, "Daniela has been immensely wealthy-far wealthier than we've realized. She isn't just the CEO of Elite Lux; she's also the one running Dreamscape."

Compared to Dreamscape, what significance did Elite Lux really hold? Perhaps to Daniela, Elite Lux was merely a minor outpost in her vast business dominion.
