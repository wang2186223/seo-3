Chapter 792

In the days that passed,Alexander wracked his mind,frantic to find a way to see Daniela

But the tight security at Elite Lux headquarters made it nearly impossible for him to reach Daniela.

The photographer, growing frustrated, asked, "Are you close to Daniela or not?"

"Of course I am!" Alexander retorted sharply.

"Then why is meeting her so difficult?" The photographer, feeling defeated, sighed. They had been waiting outside Elite Lux headquarters for days in the harsh weather "Why not just call her and suggest meeting at the cafe downstairs?"

His frustration was evident.

If Alexander was really close with Daniela, asking her to meet at the cafe seemed rather simple.

"I'll snap a few staged photos," the photographer said."I'll edit the rest. Is that acceptable?"

Alexander pressed his lips together, staring at the towering Elite Lux building,feeling defeated He had her number Yet he couldn't get through to her She hung up as soon as she recognized his voice How could he possibly get in touch with her?

Meeting her now seemed nearly impossible, let alone sitting down for coffee.

Just as Alexander was about to snap back at the photographer's criticism, Hamilton's call came through

"Can't you even manage a few photos? I didn't think you'd be this useless, Alexander. I don't care how it's done,but those photos need to be in my hands today The airspace restrictions in Oiscoll are lifted today,and Daniela is sure to return. If you don't get them today,I won't need them anymore!"

Hamilton's furious voice rang through the phone.

Alexander shut his eyes for a moment, then lifted his gaze toward the CEO's office at Elite Lux.

Several hours passed.

"Fire!" A shout rang out, and the Elite Lux security guards quickly rushed to the scene.

Alexander and the photographer took advantage of the moment, slipping into the building and heading towards the CEO's office.

The photographer let out a sigh. "No surprise she was once the world's wealthiest. The building is unbelievably opulent!"

Alexander was in awe of the breathtaking design

This was Daniela's empire, her reign in the business world

This was the Elite Lux headquarters.

It was the epitome of creativity and luxury, a place so grand it almost felt like a museum.

Alexander was deeply moved by the sight

Had he not divorced Daniela, perhaps he would have a stake here now.

As Alexander surveyed the space, the photographer realized the sheer dominance Daniela held in the business world

He was both awestruck and humbled by the sight.

His eagerness to meet Daniela surged, desperate for another shot at redemption.

His excitement surged through him as he drew closer to the CEO's office.

At that moment, the secretary emerged from the restroom and eyed Alexander and the photographer suspiciously. "Who are you? Do you have a scheduled meeting?"How did you even get up here? Security! We have unauthorized visitors! Please send help!"

Alexander quickly tried to explain, "I'm Daniela's husband, Alexander. Is shehere? I need to speak with her."

The secretary's expression hardened. "Alexander? I've never heard of you. Our boss's husband is Cedric,not Alexander If you're going to lie, at least get your facts straight!"

Alexander quickly insisted, "I was Daniela's husband,though we're divorced now. I need to speak with her.Please don't stop me."

Without hesitation, he shoved the secretary aside

The secretary stumbled, nearly losing her balance in her high heels.

Alexander rushed into the CEO's office, only to find it completely empty.

The security guards quickly arrived. The secretary pointed at Alexander, shouting, "That's him! Take him away!"

Alexander was left stunned. Turning to the secretary,he asked urgently, "Where is Daniela? I need to see her!" The secretary rolled her eyes, "Our boss left for Oiscoll" She pointed to the helicopter soaring from the balcony. "She just flew out"

Alexander stood frozen,dumbfounded.

How could she have left without a word?

How could this be possible?

He was still here, with so much left unsaid to Daniela

He hadn't even had the chance to sip a cup of coffee at the Elite Lux

How could Daniela just leave like that?

Why hadn't she told him?

"Is there another helicopter available?" Alexander asked,almost frantic. "Daniela must have forgotten to take me with her.Please, get me another helicopter!"

The photographer closed his eyes, feeling deeply embarrassed.

But Alexander wouldn't give up,pleading desperately with the secretary, "I'm telling the truth. I saw Daniela a few days ago. She knows I'm here. We were supposed to leave together. She must have forgotten.If you don't believe me, call her!"

He looked genuinely earnest, his desperation clear

The secretary hesitated for a moment before deciding to call Daniela

When the call connected, Daniela's calm voice echoed through the line."Hello."
