Under Daniela's mentorship, she had become a force to be reckoned with, both sharp and resolute.

In just a short span, her presence had significantly boosted the marketing department's performance,much to Daniela's relief.

On the celebrated day of Elite Lux's grand fashion show, a parade of top-tier models from all corners of the globe took the stage.

That momentous day, as onlookers gazed upon the breathtaking designs, it was crystal clear that Daniela,the venerable president of Elite Lux, had preserved her renowned creative spark.

Beneath the surface, unseen forces were once again in motion.

Rooted to the spot, she whispered to herself, "Cedric told me about the exceptional salmon they have today. He's whipped up some splendid sushi that he's bringing up shortly."

At that moment, Nina approached, her arms overflowing with gifts. "Many gentlemen are all keen to share a meal with Danela."

Daniela's unmatched allure in the business world was evident once more.

After all, wasn't she grappling with amnesia? And with no children to speak of, her union with Cedric was on shaky ground. The most eligible bachelors in the city were watching keenly,ready to make their advances.
