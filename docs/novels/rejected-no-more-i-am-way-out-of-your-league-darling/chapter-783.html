No official source stepped up to report the incident Still, the rumors spread quietly across the industry They were vivid, detailed, and hard to ignore

By the next morning, word had it the comatose employee had ingested food contaminated with parasites.

This wvasn't just a question of hygiene anymore.

It had turned into a full-blown food safety crisis

As one of the biggest names in Oiscoll's dining scene,McCoy Group was hit hard.

That same day alone, their dining division lost millions.

The damage didn't stop there. The trust crisis rippled outward. Other sectors took hits, too. Sales dropped across the board.

Later that night, Hamilton stopped Nikolas just as he was leaving a bar.

Hamilton handed him a report. Nikolas took one look and froze.

"This can't be right. No one should've passed out! All we did was add laxatives-to slow them down. That's it. How could someone fall unconscious? And now they're throwing around words like 'safety concerns'?They're calling for a shutdown? That's absurd! Our food uses fresh ingredients. There's no reason for this!"

His voice cracked as he turned to Hamilton. "This isn't my fault, Father. It has to be Daniela. She's the one doing this. She's framing me-trying to ruin me! You can't believe her!"

Hamilton didn't move. He stared straight at Nikolas."You still don't get it, do you? What matters isn't what you did. It's what people think you did. McCoy Group is on every move you make. That means every mistake you make stains all of us. And trust me,Daniela isn't done. This loss won't be the last."

Nikolas stood still. He didn't know what to say.

Just as Hamilton predicted, another rumor began to circulate.

They said the patient in a coma had taken a turn for the worse. His condition had grown even more serious.

Outside her company building,Daniela stood before the press. Her face was pale, her expression tight with pain. "I've never wanted much. I just hoped for a fair place in this industry. McCoy Group has ruled for years We're new, still building. Is there really no space for us?Why must it all belong to them? I used to think compromise was the answer. But after seeing my staff hurt like this, I see things differently now. I won't back down. Starting today, I'll stand at the front line. No matter the struggle, I'll grow from it. My company's chain dining business begins now We don't just take dining seriously-we treat it with care. We're not in this for prestige or profit. We're here to give every ordinary person a proper meal."

In the business world, nothing mattered more than trust.

Daniela's speech struck a chord with the public.

That same day, her dining venture launched with a roar. It entered the Oiscoll market with force and didn't look back.

"This has to be a setup! It's asetup!" Nikolas had been blindsided.

He slammed his hand on the table, his voice breaking apart. "This is all Daniela! She planned this! How else could it happen so fast? Our crisis just broke, and suddenly she's ready to open shop? She was waiting for this.Waiting to strike the moment we fell!"

Nikolas was spiraling.

Everything Daniela had done now looked like part of a trap. She had backed him into a corner, drained every ounce of leverage he had left.

As he stood there, breath ragged, something finally sank in.

Daniela had played it quiet, but she had planned it all.

He suddenly understood why Charles always flattered Daniela and why Hamilton always thought he couldn't outsmart Daniela and Cedric.

He felt like a fool standing there.

Nikolas stood in the high-rise of McCoy Group, facing the building across the street.

Daniela had made her move bold and clean. On the very first day of operation, she struck hard.McCoy Group's dining business took a hit it couldn't hide.

But Daniela wasn't done.

Whispers about McCoy Group's downfall started swirling again. Their name was losing weight fast.

Rage surged through Nikolas. He stormed into the other building and confronted Daniela.

"What will it take for you to stop?"

He hadn't expected a single misstep to cost the company this much. But Daniela wasn't even paying attention. She was buried in charts and numbers. She wasn't the one spreading rumors. It was Carol.

Daniela looked up, watching Nikolas rant in front of her. "Can't you see your own limits? You're not my opponent. You never were. If you want to talk,bring your father. Tell Hamilton to come and apologize to me. Maybe then, we'll consider moving on."
