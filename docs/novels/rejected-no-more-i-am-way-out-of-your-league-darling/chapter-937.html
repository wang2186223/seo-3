Jules' eyes drifted to the villa, then dropped to the empty paper in his hands. A restless feeling stirred in his gut.

When Jules neared the gate, he leaned close to his right-hand man and whispered, "Get some eyes on Josh. Find out what he's really doing these days." The man beside him paused, blinking hard. "You actually trust what Daniela hinted? You think Josh is hiding something? If he catches wind of this, he'll blow up for sure."

Jules's brow furrowed. "Then don't let him find out.Daniela's not the type to speak for nothing.Check it all. I want peace of mind. Also, dig into McCoy Group's books and watch for any strange money moves."

His right-hand man dipped his head. "Understood,Mr. McCoy. I'll handle it."

Only then did Jules' shoulders drop a little. He headed inside,ready to rest.

When the house grew quiet, the right-hand man slipped out the back, found a dark corner, and called a number. "MAr. McCoy, just as you guessed. Jules is suspicious after Daniela opened her mouth.

"Yes,they did."

Josh's grin grew sharp. "My dear brothers never disappoint."

Even if she carries to term,that baby won't be healthy."

Josh chuckled, voice icy. "I expect good news soon."He ended the call.

Meanwhile, back in his room, Jules watched the feeds showing Daniela's movements. A knock pulled him from the screen.

"Mr. McCoy, we ran the checks. McCoy Group's accounts look steady. The stock price keeps dropping sharply, but otherwise there's nothing strange. As for Josh, his money flow is clean. Same

A pang of guilt pressed on him. He vowed right then -no matter what came next, Josh would always have his loyalty.
