Daniela could have waited patiently for the chaos to unfold, stepping in later as the untouchable savior.

But she hadn't.

She had even given Nina an opportunity to walk away.

Why?

Nina's mind swirled with unanswered questions, but she couldn't voice them all. Daniela's expression remained impassive as she said quietly, "Because it's too dirty."

Daniela's tone didn't waver. "Using Cedric's name for something like that-it's revolting. I despise it, and l won't let it happen. Nina froze, the weight of Daniela's words sinking in."That's it? Just for that reason?"

Daniela inclined her head slightly. "That's all there is to it."

A sudden wave of emotion overcame Nina. Tears pooled in her eyes, spilling down her cheeks-not out of heartbreak, but from an overwhelming sense of gratitude.

Because, at last, she had found someone who held love in its purest, untainted form-just as she did.

Nina had thought offering herself up for Cedric's sake was already a monumental sacrifice.

But Daniela's ability to cast aside her disdain, all in the name of true love, felt even more extraordinary.

After all, Nina had faltered halfway through her plan,while Daniela had gone through with saving her.

Meeting Daniela's gaze with newfound determination, Nina spoke with quiet resolve."Daniela, you've proven your strength. Whatever your plans are, I'm on your side now. I'll be the weapon you need to destroy every threat the Dury family poses!"

He smiled shamelessly at the thought, his ego inflating as he got dressed.

Linden headed back to the hospital, his mind set on fulfilling his promise to finish Cedric off.

But as he approached the hospital's entrance, he froze, his gaze catching sight of several medical staff rolling their suitcases away. Confused, he quickened his steps and called out,"What's going on? Why are you all leaving?"

One nurse paused, glancing over her shoulder. "Ms.Harper ordered a complete replacement of the staff this morning. All of us have been dismissed."

For a brief moment, Linden's confidence faltered.Panic rippled through him, tightening his chest.

He hadn't even begun to make his move.

If he left now, there was no guarantee Natalie would ever speak to him again.

But even more pressing was the sudden image of Daniela's striking face flashing through his mind.

The idea was intoxicating, and he couldn't hold back the surge of excitement flooding through him.

Everything in his life seemed to be aligning perfectly lately.
