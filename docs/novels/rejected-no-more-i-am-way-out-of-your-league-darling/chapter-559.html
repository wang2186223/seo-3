As the post gained traction, employees from Phillips Group and Elite Lux scrambled to refocus the public's wrath on Cedric, dismissing the revelation as mere distraction. "Ignore this rubbish! Stay focused on denouncing Cedric!"

Observers suspected a deflection tactic and escalated their insults. Simultaneously intrigued, they began tracking the whistleblower.

Moments later, the anonymous source uploaded a blurry video.

It depicted a man in a suit, departing the Bennett family's residence and proceeding to a press event. He wore a mask,yet his build bore a striking resemblance to Cedric.

This left everyone puzzled and questioning.

Phillips Group and Elite Lux employees maintained their stance. "Stay focused! Continue to target Cedric!"

However,skepticism was mounting.

Questions arose about why Cedric would be seen leaving Alexander's residence at such a late hour.

These suspicions, combined with earlier rumors about plastic surgery, sparked a wave of curiosity.
