Daniela's grin carried a hint of irony. It was cold,almost chilly.

Gulping,Linden stammered, "Daniela, no, Ms. Harper,I need to explain."

He had long viewed Daniela as merely a wealthy but delicate woman. Despite her assertive tactics,he thought it was her team that truly wielded the power.allowing her to achieve her goals.

Without her team, he believed Daniela would be powerless.

Linden had even entertained the thought that Daniela's disappearance would go unnoticed.

Yet, in this moment, he realized his misconception. A woman who had climbed to the CEO position of a top global firm must possess significant personal strength.

Her striking features had obscured the evident truths.

Though Linden was driven by desire, he valued his life above all.

Desperately. Linden explained, "Ms. Harper, I assure you, I had no part in this. I wanted Nina. I was clueless about why Elyse dragged you here instead. Elyse is greedy.but I am not involved."

Daniela's expression did not waver. She idly played with a knife, her lips curving into a mysterious, subtle smile.

"Ms.Harper,wvhat are you implying? Please, I'm telling the truth. Check my phone. It was Elyse,that deceitful woman, who couldn't locate Nina and substituted you because you were available. This wasn't my plan."

Only in his underpants. Linden shivered as the cold air rushed in from the open window. Daniela had tied him to a chair.

"Ms.Harper.please can you say something?"

Daniela released a chilling laugh. She gazed at Linden and whispered,"Did you know? When wealthy people commit murder,someone always cleans up. It's like it never happened."

Linden's face paled, his eyes wide with fear.

"Elyse mentioned she couldn't find Nina,but found me instead. You were okay with that, weren't you?"she added.

Linden didn't know how to defend himself this time."1...”

"How would you like to meet your end? It's your choice," Daniela murmured with a smile that was both alluring and dangerous, like a beautiful yet deadly rose, leaving Linden frozen with fear.

"Do you prefer falling from the 48th floor or perhaps drowning in a bathtub suits you better? Or shall we arrange an accidental electrocution? That last one's quick-over in seconds. You wouldn't feel a thing.What do you think?"

Daniela's manner of listing the death options was as casual as one might discuss the day's weather.

Shaking uncontrollably, Linden pleaded, "Ms. Harper.please understand,I had no part in this.Elyse promised me time with Nina in exchange for a favor,but murder was never my intention,I promise you that!"

A slight smile played on Daniela's lips, her voice light "Really now?"

Linden gave a frantic nod. Without a word, Daniela pulled out her phone and began to play back recordings of every conversation Linden had shared with Elyse and Natalie.

Linden's eyes bulged with realization as he faced Daniela. "You've known everything all this time! Then why...?”

With a soft laugh, Daniela replied, "Why did I allow myself to be brought here today? I was curious to see whether you were behind the attempt on my life or if it was those two women."

A cold shiver ran through Linden. Stuttering,he insisted, "It wasn't me, I swear. I would never dare." Daniela eyed him, amusement in her gaze. "Really?"

Again. Linden nodded eagerly. "Absolutely! I am at your command. Whatever you need, I'll do it. It's them -they're the threat,not me. They wanted to kill you!Ms. Harper, let me assist you with your investigation.Just,please,spare my life."

Again,Daniela's chuckle was soft yet chilling.

pulling up live feeds from the hotel's room camera.
