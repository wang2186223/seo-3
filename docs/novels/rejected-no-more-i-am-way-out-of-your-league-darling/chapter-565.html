Chapter 561

Alexander let his gaze linger on Daniela's face before it unintentionally drifted to her abdomen. It was a brief moment before he looked away.

"Daniela, perhaps I should go with you to the hospital? Stomach troubles are not to be taken lightly," Alexander suggested, his tone laden with concern.

Daniela headed for the door, replying dismissively,"That won't be necessary. I already have a family doctor."

Alexander trailed her to the staircase, his eyes following her departure, continuing to stare long after she had disappeared.

"Norton, come here," Alexander commanded with gravity.

"Mr. Bennett, what can I do for you?" Norton Truman asked promptly.

"Do you still have that cousin working at Elite Lux?"

A nod from Norton confirmed it.

"Good. I need him to monitor Daniela's health closely."

Norton's expression clouded with confusion.

Alexander instructed, "Check if she's pregnant."

If she was, he would need to tamper with her food to prevent any possibility of a child.

Daniela belonged to him alone!

If she ever had a child, it would be his and no one else's!

The thought of her bearing a child that wasn't his filled him with a violent jealousy.

All this turmoil was Joyce's doing!

Had Joyce not interfered, he would never have lost Daniela. The secretary observed a frightening malice flash in Alexander's eyes.

In a dark villa basement at night, Joyce lay bloodied and barely conscious in a pool of her own blood.Alexander, finished with his brutal assault, tossed the whip aside with scorn.

He then stepped on Joyce's face, pressing down with his shoe. "You liked the scent of blood, didn't you? Well, now's your chance to indulge!"

His shoe pressed hard against Joyce's blood-streaked face, as he said coldly, "Joyce, your end is deserved."

The night dragged on, filled with the harsh sounds of a whip cracking.

Feeling nearly lifeless, Joyce was barely conscious when an elderly man intervened, pulling Alexander away by his arm.

With a mocking sneer, Alexander departed without looking back. In the dim light, Joyce, battered and weak, secretly grasped a small shard of glass in her hand.

Daniela had just walked out of Alexander's hotel,when she got a piece of news.

Natalie had returned as a celebrated international actress. She had headed straight for Elite Lux,insistently asking to meet with Daniela.

Concern etched on Lillian's face as she fretted over Daniela's well-being.

Daniela said, "Have her come to the house."

Acknowledging with a nod, Lillian recalled that Cedric was currently away, tied up with resolving financial upheavals, thus leaving their residence unoccupied.

After arriving home, Daniela was about to refresh herself with a shower.

Before going to the bathroom, she turned to Lillian and instructed, "Call Elyse over."

Lillian's gaze lingered on Daniela, her voice tinged with concern. "Won't it escalate the situation?"

Daniela's smile widened. "Exactly, what a spectacle it will be to watch them clash!"

With a laugh, Lillian agreed.

Fresh from her shower, Daniela descended the stairs to find Natalie already waiting.

Natalie now exuded with the poise and presenceof a movie star.

During her time abroad, Natalie voiced a character in a critically acclaimed animated film, connected with a top director, and skillfully secured a role in a blockbuster movie. Her return was marked by significant media attention.

Now established by international fame and swollen with pride, she had become bold.

"I have no hidden agendas, Daniela. I aim to re-establish my career here. You control several entertainment outlets. Hand one over to me; I'll manage it," she said boldly. Daniela quietly filled a glass with water.

"Alternatively, allocate some resources to me. I've learned that your company holds the rights to Ultimate Kung Fu. I want the lead role in that movie,"Natalie continued.

Daniela grasped the full extent of Natalie's ambitions.

Ultimate Kung Fù, a martial arts epic, featured a love story that challenged traditional boundaries. The protagonist was a heroic and stunning woman,battling sinister forces with bravery and virtue.

She made the ultimate sacrifice, staying true to the justice she cherished until the end.

This pitiful conclusion left countless fans longing for a film version wwhere the heroine might find a happier fate alongside her beloved.

The announcement of the film's production had drawn the attention of many renowned actresses, all eager to secure the lead role.

Aware of this desired opportunity, Natalie had made her way back to the country specifically for this purpose.

Yet, she mentioned the role nonchalantly upon arrival, seemingly taking for granted Daniela's potential unawareness of the film's deep significance.

Daniela could only smirk in response. Clearly, Natalie had underestimated her.

Turning her gaze to the guest room, Daniela said, "I'need some time to consider this."
