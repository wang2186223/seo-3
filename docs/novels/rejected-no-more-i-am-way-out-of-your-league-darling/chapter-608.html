Chapter 604

The days began to settle into a serene rhythm.

As Daniela's pregnancy advanced, her belly became noticeably larger.

The first trimester brought with it severe morning sickness, and she lost a notable amount of weight,which deeply troubled Cedric.

Determined to ease her discomfort, Cedric became creative in the kitchen, whipping up nourishing meals for Daniela. He also took on more responsibilities at Elite Lux to reduce her workload.

By the fifth month of her pregnancy, Cedric had purchased an island that offered a consistent climate,surrounded by mountains and waters, ideal for a peaceful getaway.

Daniela concluded her duties at the company, gearing up for a vacation with Cedric.

"Love, I need to sign a contract at the office this afternoon.It shouldn't take longer than an hour. Wait for me at home, and we'll head to the airport right after," Cedric told her. Having already packed for both of them, Daniela settled comfortably on the couch to watch TV. "Take care driving," she reminded him.

Cedric walked away with a satisfied smile, warmth lingering in his expression.

Ever since Daniela became pregnant, his days had been filled with joy and anticipation, eagerly counting down to the moment their child would arrive.

After Cedric left, Ryan was alerted by the company about a glitch in the security system, prompting him to leave their home.

At the same time, Lillian and Josie were at the vet,sorting out an issue with unavailable prescribed dog food.

As a result, Daniela was left alone in the house.

Suddenly,her phone rang.

As she bent down to find it, a cold breeze swept through, and the door creaked open as if pushed by the wind.

Rising cautiously, Daniela spotted a shadowy figure Covered in black, donning a black hat, at the doorway.

She narrowed her eyes at the intruder."It's you?" Daniela attempted to evade the blow, but her pregnant state hindered her agility.

A sharp pain shot through her shoulder, followed by the overpowering smell of a noxious substance,causing her to faint.

When consciousness returned, she found herself confined in the storage room outside the villa.

Her wrists were bound to the bedpost, and the room reeked of gasoline.

Just as she gathered the breath to scream, the figure in black flicked a lighter, igniting the room in flames.

Instantly, the room was consumed by fire.

As the fire raged around her, Daniela's coughs filled the air, desperate and harsh.

The figure watched from outside, his voice cold and threatening. "Daniela, this is your doing. You were to0much of a threat!"

"Who are you?"

From afar, Cedric noticed someone lurking outside the storage room.

He slammed on the accelerator, jumped from his car, saw a figure scaling the wall.

He wanted to chase him but was halted by Daniela's faint cries. With a heavy heart, he dashed towards the blazing flames.

Moments later, the sound of an explosive boom shattered the air.

Days passed.

"Daniela Harper,CEO of Elite Lux, died in a horrific fire.Cedric Phillips of Phillips Group is critically injured and remains unconscious," said the news.

Time moved on.

"In the wake of Daniela Harper's death, Elite Lux has halted all domestic operations, with Cedric Phillips still in a coma."

Weeks turned into months.

"Devastated by his wife's death, Cedric Phillips resigns as CEO of Phillips Group."

Alexander stood in front of the towering Elite Lux building,his heart heavy with loss.

He had hoped his efforts would mend things between them,allowing for a new beginning with Daniela. He could never have foreseen such a catastrophic conclusion.

Night after night, Alexander sought refuge in bars,nursing his grief.

Three years passed.

He stumbled upon a fresh advertisement from Elite Lux.

Something about the image struck him-a silhouette that seemed eerily familiar.
