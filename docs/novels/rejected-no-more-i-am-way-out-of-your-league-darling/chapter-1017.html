Chapter 1012

Hamilton initially planned to restrain himself, but ultimately failed.

Jules sounded like he care about Daniela.Nevertheless, it merely served as a justification to confirm whether Daniela was poisoned.

Jules articulated everything so sweetly, yet none of his brothers demonstrated as much devotion toward Daniela as Carol did.

They were brothers, but now it seemed that bond wasn't important at all.

Their bond couldn't even compared to what Daniela and Carol shared.

Contemplating that, Hamilton balled his hands ferociously and outright launched verbal attacks at Jules.

"Aren't you ashamed? You're already adults, but lack financial capability. You avoid addressing your responsibilities, but constantly hassle my daughter-in -law. I don't get why guilt never strikes you. Why don't you just die?"

Jules stared at him, stunned. "Hamilton..."

"Enough nonsense!" Hamilton roared."Listen carefully.I once endured all of you because I trusted familial solidarity. I merely assumed we belonged together.Before Father died, he told me to look after you guys.Now I realize you guys are quite capable and don't need my care at all. Starting today, we go our separate ways. You affairs have nothing to do with me anymore."

Saying that, Hamilton grabbed Jules' bag and flung it onto the ground, trampling over it repeatedly before entering the house furiously.

Jules remained immobilized. He questioned reality,uncertain if the person earlier truly was Hamilton.Wasn't Hamilton always the most forgiving among the family? Why couldn't he endure them any further?

Josh stepped from the vehicle, his expression icy as he commented, "Hamilton isn't the same guy as before. Have you realized that now?"

Jules scoffed, "What a scoundrel! Just because he's got grandkids now, he forgets his siblings,huh?"

Josh, craving chaos, replied, "Daniela has snatched everything meant for you. Now that he's rich,Hamilton has naturally become smug, dismissing his struggling kin."

Jules burned inside, his glare venomous as Hamilton's figure vanished into the house.

That day, Daniela remained unharmed.

Josh restlessly lingered until twilight. As Jules' concern intensified, the front door of the house gradually creaked open.

Daniela appeared at the door, clad in a flowing white dress,beaming warmly and waving at the two men.

Josh, positioned in the vehicle, glanced at Jules,confused. "Is she waving at us?"

Jules faltered. "No way. She can't spot us from that distance."

On hearing it, Josh redirected his attention to Daniela."Maybe Hamilton entered and informed Daniela we're outside. That's why she is inviting us in."

Josh nudged the car open, tone quiet and ominous,whispering to Jules, "Let's get inside. We'll confirm if Daniela is poisoned. If not, I'll poison her again. This evening, she and her unborn babies must perish."

Josh and Jules exited the car and approached,forcing grins onto their expressions.

Unexpectedly, Daniela's grin outshone theirs.

"Just wait. You'll stop grinning soon," Josh muttered inwardly.

"After all that cash you stole from me, you still beam like that. Today, you'll die," Jules, too, whispered under his breath
