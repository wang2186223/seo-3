Cedric turned around, only to see the usual diners eating and chatting.

Daniela pulledthe man into the restroom and, before stepping in, hung up a "Under Maintenance" sign on the door.

The man was caught off guard at first, but once he saw Daniela standing in front of him, he seemed to calm down a little.

Despite her immense power and influence, Daniela was alone at that moment. No matter what went down, it wouldn't be anything too serious. The pay for this gig was huge. Even if he lost the job, it wouldn't matter.

He was shocked by her sudden concession, unsure of what to make of her calm attitude.
