Daniela's eyes followed Hamilton as he slid into his vehicle across the street.

Yes. That was exactly her intention.

Since Hamilton had no involvement in her mother's death, she was willing to give him a chance to break off from the McCoy family. She intended for him to grasp just how corrupt the family truly was.

Daniela's reply was cool and measured. "That's his burden, Extending the offer was my call."

After all, Hamilton was Cedric's father. She was willing to give the old man another chance.
