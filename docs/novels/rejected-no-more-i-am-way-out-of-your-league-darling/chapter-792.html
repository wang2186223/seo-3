Daniela gave a silent nod in response. With that,Damon took his leave.

Roughly thirty minutes passed before Nikolas showed up. Without saying much, he handed Daniela a blank check.

"What's this supposed to mean?" Daniela raised a brow,confused by the gesture.

"I'll cover whatever expenses you've got. Any damages, all of it. Consider it handled."

That only made her more skeptical. She studied his face but couldn't read his intent.

Nikolas' voice didn't waver. "My father won't let this go.You need to leave Oiscoll now. I know why you came-to dig into the explosion and the person behind it. Give me a little time. I'll find out who's responsible and deal with them myself. But you have to walk away. Today. And don't come back." Daniela's gaze fell to the check again. She let out a soft, amused laugh. "Let me guess You're scared Cedric might change his mind? Worried he'll come back and take his place in the McCoy family? That your claimn to the throne isn't so solid after all?"

The words landed harder than Nikolas expected.

There was a time when he had already written off the chance of inheriting McCoy Group. Cedric had given him a shot by stepping down. And if that chance existed, then he'd have to fight to protect it.

"You're not wrong," Nikolas said at last. There was no use pretending. Daniela would see through it anyway."I'm not like Cedric. I'm not easygoing, and I'm not willing to step aside. I've spent everything I have trying to secure that future. People are supporting me-people who've put their trust in me. I can't afford to lose this. But I give you my word-I'll find whoever's behind the explosion. I'll make sure justice is served."

For a moment, Daniela believed him. There was something different in his eyes. A fire that hadn't been there before. But instead of accepting, she shook her head.

"What's that supposed to mean?" Nikolas asked,tension rising. "You're staying? You've got another plan? Or are you hiding some angle with the McCoy family I don't know about?" Daniela met his eyes with a calm,almost amused smile "I've got no interest in the McCoy family's inheritance What I question is whether you have what it takes."

That hit harder than expected. Nikolas looked thrown."What's that supposed to mean?"

"Let's just say your recent behavior hasn't exactly earned you Hamilton's confidence. If anything,it casts doubt on whether he still supports you at all. And if by some miracle you do end up with the title, would you even have the guts to go after the real culprit?"

Nikolas opened his mouth, likely to offer some promise or reassurance.

But she shut it down before he could speak."What if the person behind it all is your father? You still willing to play hero then?"

Nikolas's expression turned cold. "There's no way it's him."

"Nothing's off the table. You know that. And I doubt you'd have the spine to face that truth. Believe this or don't-it makes no difference to me. Cedric and I have no desire for your family's wealth or power.I've said this before, but this will be the last time I explain myself. Don't show up offering terms you aren't qualified to make. I always settle my debts."

Whatever confidence Nikolas had walked in with had vanished He turned away shoulders heavier than when he arrived.

Carol shook her head and commented, "That guy? All ambition, no skill. He'll never get the McCoy fortune."

Even Hamilton knew that. If Nikolas had truly been capable, there would've been no need to chase after Cedric in the first place.
