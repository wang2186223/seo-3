"Besides,Alexander,you'venevertruly understoodme,"Daniela added.

"Do you honestly think you know me? I'm not the kind of person who dwells on the past-be it people or things.I married Cedric because I genuinely love him.Haven't you ever wondered how I can always tell that you're not Cedric?"

"Why?" Alexander asked, his gaze locked on hers.

"Because I love him. The first clue was a scar. Then,there was a habit of yours that didn't match. And over time, even the smallest gesture of yours was enough for me to recognize that you weren't Cedric." Alexander's eyes went wide with shock. "A scar?Where is it? No way! The plastic surgeon made sure l was an exact replica of Cedric, down to the smallest detail!"

Daniela sighed, shaking her head. She had said plenty,yet he only clung to what suited him.

Alexander tensed, as though he'd just uncovered a vital secret.

So that was it-Daniela saw through him because of a scar he didn't even know existed. If he recreated that scar, he could deceive her once more. He could reclaim her smile, her protection-everything.

Consumed by desperation, Alexander tugged his hat down further, as if trying to disappear from sight.

Snatching the bank transfer device from the table, he thrust it into Daniela's hand and rushed out without another word.

He would leave the country, undergo another surgery.He would become Cedric-the man Daniela loved-through and through.

Just before departing, Alexander shot a message to the company's finance team.

"No matter how much is spent, don't report it to me.My wife is the one using the money." The finance team confirmed the instruction but couldn't help feeling confused. His wife? Did he mean Joyce?

Daniela brushed Alexander off and headed upstairs to play cards with Ryan and Lillian.

But they quickly found out their efforts were useless -there was no reaction at all.

Meanwhile, Elite Lux held a groundbreaking fashion show.

Stunning models showcased exquisite designs on the nation's most prestigious stage, captivating the audience.

The public remained indifferent to the intense corporate warfare.

The fashion show turned out to be a spectacular triumph.

Elite Lux celebrated its most remarkable success in years,raking in tens of billions in on-site orders.

With this single move, the association's embargo became meaningless. One by one, members started silently pulling out of the association.
