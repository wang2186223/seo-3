Chapter 387

With a satisfied smile, Nina exited, leaving Natalie frozen in place. Her eyes lingered through the door,fixed on Daniela in the CEO's office, a sneer curling her lips.

Before leaving for a meeting, Cedric had kissed Daniela on the forehead.

Natalie turned to Daniela, her voice dripping with mockery. "I must say, I envy the bond you have with Cedric. Not many men are like him. Even when you insist it's just about the physical,he still stays."

Understanding the subtle challenge in Natalie's words,Daniela chose silence, waiting for more.

Natalie paused for a few moments, waiting for a response from Daniela.When none came,she smirked and let out a chuckle. "Daniela, I just overheard Cedric planning to meet Nina this weekend."

Daniela remained emotionless. When it came to attraction, she was completely sure of herself. She trusted in her appearance and Cedric's loyalty.

Natalie felt a twinge of disappointment, unable to sense any emotion from Daniela.

Did Daniela truly not care about Cedric?

Leaning in slightly, Natalie couldn't resist pressing further. "Aren't you curious about what they'll be up to this weekend?"

She waited, the silence thick between them.

Daniela's gaze remained locked on the project proposal on her screen, completely ignoring Natalie's question.

There was no flicker of concern for Cedric in her expression either.

Natalie clenched her jaw in quiet frustration, lowering her eyes as she made her way out of the office.

In the hallway, she called her mother. "Mom, stop trying. I've tested her again and again. Daniela doesn't care about Cedric. If you want to hit her where it hurts,you'll have to dig into her personal affairs. Cedric won't work."

Elyse's voice crackled with doubt through the phone."Are you sure? Nina mentioned things were heating up between them. Cedric is Daniela's weakness, her Achilles' heel. Do you really think you've got it right?"

"Mom, do you not trust me?" Natalie's tonewas tinged with impatience. "I told you, they only have a physical connection. If she truly cared, she'd be worried about him meeting someone else. Daniela doesn't care for Cedric at all."

On the other end, Elyse exhaled sharply, her voice laced with concern. "If that's the case, we're in trouble.Without a weakness like Cedric, we'll have to go after herself to get to her assets."

Though she spoke with resolve, unease lingered in Elyse's voice.

Determined, she made her decision.

With a small gift in hand, she stepped into Daniela's office.

A smile played on her lips as she said, "Daniela, you know, Nina's getting older. It's time she thought about marriage."

The moment Elyse spoke, Daniela knew exactly what was coming.

Without even lifting her gaze, she responded casually,"Oh? Do you have someone in mind?"

Elyse didn't hesitate. "Of course. Nina's particular,you see. After all this time working as your secretary, she's taken a liking to one of the men over at Elite Lux. But she's worried you won't approve."

A faint smile tugged at Daniela's lips. "Surely you're joking. The employees at Elite Lux are free to make their own choices when it comes to marriage."

Elyse took a slow sip of her coffee, her laughter gentle but knowing. "This one's a bit different, though.Your approval is important."

Daniela, still engrossed in her work, gave a brief nod."I'm listening."

Without hesitation, Elyse dropped the name. "Cedric."

"Cedric?" Daniela's pen hovered over the contract as she glanced down, her expression unreadable.

"Yes, Cedric. I'm here to ask if what you have with him is serious, or just a passing fling. If it's the latter, I think it would be best to let Nina and Cedric have their chance together." A knowing smile crept onto Elyse's face. "Cedric has a lot of potential. If he were to marry into the Dury family, he'd be quite the asset in the future,wouldn't you agree?"

Elyse was certain her question would make Daniela hesitant, expecting some vague or unclear answer.

"I've slept with Cedric," Daniela said.
