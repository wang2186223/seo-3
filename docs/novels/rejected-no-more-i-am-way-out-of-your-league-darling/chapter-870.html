Was Hamilton really the only one who knew the truth about her mother's death?

Maybe someone else inside McCoy Group knew. If that was true, she would have two options.

She could join McCoy Group, working from within to get closer to the truth

The other route-launching an all-out business assault-would force Hamilton's hand, driving him to the edge until he finally cracked and confessed

That second option, though, would rip away any last hint of civility between her and Hamilton.

No matter how hard she tried to deny it,Daniela had always pulled back just a little. Cedric's connection to the McCoy family kept her from going too far

The day Hamilton demanded she leave Cedric, her answer hadn't come instantly. Deep down, she wanted to shield Cedric from the storm.

Now, everything had changed. Cedric would never accept being protected on the sidelines.

After turning the situation over in her mind,Daniela realized there was only one real path left. She would have to see just how much pressure Hamilton could take before he finally gave up the truth

Meanwhile, Hamilton's anger was impossible to hide. If Daniela wanted a fight, he was more than willing to give her one
