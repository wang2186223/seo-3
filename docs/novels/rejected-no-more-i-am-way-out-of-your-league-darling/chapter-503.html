Chapter 499

Daniela swung open her bedroom door and was instantly enveloped in a tight embrace. A soft chuckle escaped her.

Cedric clung to her, murmuring, "Daniela, you are my greatest love in this entire world."

He was truly jealous. Triggered by a cryptic text,he spent all night murmuring sweet words to her.

Upon rising the next day, Daniela felt her legs wobble.

Opening the door, she was shocked to see Ryan. and Lillian there, arms folded.

"Don't you two have anything better to do than eavesdrop outside my room?" she asked.

Ryan shook his head. "We've been deceived!"

Lillian's face was grave as she said, "It's true what they say, the deepest stabs come from the closest knives."

Ryan nodded vigorously.

Confusion clouded Daniela's face.

She stepped back into her room, Ryan and Lillian trailing behind.

"We've uncovered three things."

Daniela filled a glass with water, waiting for them to continue.

"Firstly, Joyce is the one who sent money to the Lone Wolf. She's after Cedric."C

Daniela's brow furrowed. "Eight hundred million to get Cedric? So soon after Doug died? Is she that obsessed with Cedric?"

Lillian agreed. "She couldn't have him before, and now with money, she's hell-bent on getting him."

Daniela laughed lightly. "And the second revelation?"

"l tracked Joyce's finances and discovered she funded Alexander's plastic surgery. There were multiple payments between them."

Daniela nodded as she listened. "Given what Alexander revealed yesterday, everything adds up.Joyce is desperate for Cedric, while Alexander..."

Lillian added eagerly, "He's interested in you!"

Daniela paused to take another sip of water. "So,what's the third thing?"

Lillian's expression turned serious, her brow furrowing. "The identity of the Lone Wolf's leader remains a mystery, but our findings suggest their frequent activities in Olisvine. Interestingly, although their forces surround our villa, they don't appear to be threatening."

Daniela raised an eyebrow."How so?"

Ryan explained, "Their actions don't suggest they want to harm you. It's almost as if they're here to protect you."

Lillian shared his confusion. Historically, Daniela's group and the Lone Wolf had been adversaries.

Their leadership changed abruptly five years ago and they seemed to dissolve, leading everyone to believe the Lone Wolf had disbanded.

Now, they resurfaced out of nowhere, accepted Joyce's eight hundred million, and yet they remain inactive.

It was strange.

Daniela gave Ryan an instruction. "Investigate further. Return to the office and dig up whatever you can about the Lone Wolf."

It was rare for Daniela to feel this curious about anyone.

Cedric walked in just as Daniela uttered "Lone Wolf."He paused briefly.

"What wolf?" Cedric asked, stepping into the room.

Ryan and Lillian exited. Daniela offered a warm smile. "For the time being,avoid returning to Phillips Group. I've instructed the secretary to transfer your belongings to Elite Lux.You'll be sharing an office with me from now on."

Cedric was clearly delighted with the arrangement.

Later at the office, Daniela powered up her computer to delve into the Lone Wolf's background.

Just as she logged in, a loud cry disrupted the silence.

Nina's voice came through, apologetic. "I'm sorry,but without an appointment, Ms. Harper is unavailable."

A sharp slap resonated.

Elyse's furious tone filled the air, "Nina! Have you lost your senses? Your sister is in deep trouble, and this is your attitude? It seems like you take pleasure in her suffering.I swear I will make you regret this!"

The sound of slapping echoed again.

Lillian rushed outside and helped Nina off the floor. Nina stepped aside with her face covered, shot a scornful look at Elyse.

"How can you resort to violence at Elite Lux? Elyse Dury, don't you respect Ms. Harper at all? This is unacceptable!" Lillian spat.

Elyse straightened her attire. "Where's Daniela? I'm not speaking to anyone else. I need to see Daniela!"

Lillian folded her arms. "She's occupied at the moment and cannot meet with anyone."

Elyse's anxiety spiked instantly.

Natalie had turned from a well-known socialite to someone universally scorned. Without Daniela's help,her life would be ruined.

Doug was a married man, and Joyce had been causing chaos online, further tarnishing Natalie's reputation.

Joyce's tearful face only fueled public disdain for Natalie.

The backlash was severe. People were so repulsed they pelted Natalie with eggs in public.

How could Natalie withstand this?

"I am Daniela's aunt! You have no right to block me!"Elyse exclaimed loudly, her desperation clear. She believed her and Natalie's suffering justified their demands on others' empathy.

Daniela, hearing the commotion, set her computer aside and instructed, "Let her in."

Elyse, adjusting her hair, stormed into Daniela's office, casting a threatening look at Nina."You'll regret this once I'm done here!"

Upon entering, Elyse noticed Cedric at another desk,deeply engrossed in his work.

She snorted through her nose.

Natalie wouldn't have lost her mind if it hadn't been for this troublesome man.

It all started because of Cedric!

With a fierce glare at Cedric, she made her way towards Daniela.
