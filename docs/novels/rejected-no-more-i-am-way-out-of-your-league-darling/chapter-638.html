Days passed, yet Cedric never once set foot in Elite Lux.

He was consumed with the weight of Josie's funeral arrangements.

He couldn't make sense of it-why had Josie chosen to end her life so suddenly?

Inside Elite Lux, Carol asked, "Daniela, Josie took her own life. Should we take back the assets you gave her family?"

Daniela's gaze drifted to the horizon. "Her family should not bear the consequences of her actions."

Carol hesitated before asking, "Should we tell Cedric?"

Daniela's voice was void of emotion. "Unnecessary."She was someone without a past now, and Josie's death carried no weight to her.

But for Cedric, the pain would be unbearable.

Someone he had trusted like family had been the cause of his ruin. The guilt would eat at him, making him question if he could ever trust again. For over two decades, Josie had been more than a caretaker-she had been family.

And yet, he was just another casualty in this twisted game.

Carol studied Daniela's sharp profile, realizing she had offered Cedric an unusual mercy.

Daniela had endured the turmoil of losing billions,the agony of never bearing children, and te void of three lost years.

Yet she had brushed over Cedric's part in the explosion wth Josie's death.

Carol had the question on the tip of her tongue.

If Daniela loved him so deeply, why not continue to stay together?

It was obvious-Cedric still loved Daniela with every fiber of his being.

But the emptiness in Daniela's eyes made it clear-no one could reach her now. And so, Carol swallowed her question.

"Daníela, should we keep digging?"

In the end, as Elite Lux stores sprang up in prime locations nationwide, he surrendered to the undeniable force of wealth.

Step one, win her heart. Their fortune would need an heir eventually. If he had kids with someone else,he could pass them off as adopted-Daniela might even pity him for it.

Just picturing Daniela's beauty sent a rush through him,making his pulse quicken.

"Fine! Let's make it happen!"
