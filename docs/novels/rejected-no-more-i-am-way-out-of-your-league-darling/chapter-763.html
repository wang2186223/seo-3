Chapter 759

Damon tugged at his collar, his frustration apparent as he fidgeted with it.

His gaze locked onto Daniela with a determined intensity, refusing to look away.

"You're really Shim, aren't you?" he demanded, his voice firm and insistent.

Daniela gave a simple nod. "I am."

A spark of excitement flickered in Damon's eyes.

He parted his lips, eager to say more, but Nikolas yanked him aside, making him stumble slightly.

When he regained his footing, Daniela had already reached the door.

Without thinking, he hurried after her..

The entire McCoy family watched as Damon pursued Daniela, their eyes following his every move.

They all assumed he was simply unable to accept defeat.

Daniela's car sped off into the distance, but Damon stood frozen, staring after her.

Someone approached him, offering some consolation. "Damon, it's not the end. You lost this time, but you'll win next time."

The words were meant to soothe him. But Damon didn't brush it off.

His expression turned serious as he replied, "No. With my skills, I'll never be able to beat Shim."

The person stared at him in disbelief."What?"

Damon opened his mouth to explain further, but Nikolas yanked him away, the others quickly crowding around him. Nikolas snapped, "What are you saying? You'll never win? You should've accused Daniela of cheating!"

Kohen scratched his head in irritation. "Exactly! And you even chased after her. Haven't you embarrassed yourself enough?"

Charles added with a frown, "I noticed Dad's face looked terrible."

Duran muttered, "We're definitely in for a lecture when we get home."

Damon, however, seemed lost in his thoughts, his gaze fixed on the horizon where the car had vanished,paying no mind to their chatter.

The following morning, Daniela was getting ready for her visit to McCoy Group.

Cedric trailed closely behind her.

"You're truly not going to let me return to the McCoy family?" he inquired.

Daniela asked,"Why would you return? They are a bunch of idiots."

Cedric let out a soft laugh.

As they glanced ahead, Damon stood at the entrance of McCoy Group.

Cedric narrowed his eyes and stepped protectively in front of Daniela.

Damon offered a small smile, tilting his head as he addressed Daniela. "Shim... Or should I call you Daniela?Or perhaps dear sister-in-law?"

Daniela sighed in resignation.

Cedric narrowed his gaze. "Call her Daniela."

Damon managed a smile and asked, "Would you mind signing an autograph for me?"

Every employee of McCoy Group turned their attention to them.

Most assumed Damon had come to confront Daniela, perhaps to settle old scores. But when he pulled out an autograph book and approached her with an almost fan-like respect, everyone was stunned.

Before Daniela could respond, Nikolas stepped out from the building, his gaze icy as he remarked, "I didn't expect you to arrive so soon."

Daniela shot him a frosty smile, "I come when I feel like it. Is that a problem? Oh wait, I forgot-McCoy Group isn't yours yet."

"How dare you!" Nikolas snarled, his anger making veins pulse on his forehead. "Daniela, don't get too cocky! It was just a petty game yesterday. Do you really think you're so impressive? This is a global corporation, not a playground. You might want to stop before you make a fool of yourself!"

Daniela let out a light laugh. "Is that right?

If you think I'm powerlessand blind to everything, why are you so terrified of me?"

Nikolas' fury flared instantly.

Daniela smirked, lifting her chin with defiance. "Step aside. Don't stand in my way."
