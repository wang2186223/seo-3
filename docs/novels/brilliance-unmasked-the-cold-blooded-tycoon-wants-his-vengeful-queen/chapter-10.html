And what was up with Daisy? It felt like she had flipped into a totally different person overnight.

Elissa could feel her carefully built image falling apart in real time.

"That two-faced snake," she thought,seething.

Desperate to get answers, she called her mom,but the second the call connected, she was hit with the sound of sobbing. "Mom?" Elissa's voice sharpened. "What's going on?"

"I can't explain it," Meagan replied. "Your dad and I are going to talk to them.Elissa,come with us if you can." Of course, Meagan had her own plan brewing.

A shadow passed over Elissa's face as she picked up her phone and began typing, her fingers moving fast and furiously.

Meanwhile, in the quiet hospital room, Arabella sat beside Daisy, watching her silently.

Daisy's short hair was uneven and tangled,and her face was still covered in bruises. Even with all the care Arabella had given her, she still looked beaten and fragile. Arabella reached out and softly touched Daisy's cold cheek, not even realizing the tears that had started sliding down her own face.

Suddenly, Daisy's phone lit up with a buzz.

It was an old phone, clearly a few generations behind. Arabella struggled briefly before managing to open the video message.

Joyce rolled her eyes. "Elissa, you're too soft.Honestly, where would you be without us? I'm teaching this little bitch a lesson today-rip it all off!"

out laughing, but it stayed trained on Daisy.

"Please! Stop! I'm begging you!" Her desperate pleas only seemed to egg them on further.

Joyce howled with laughter, while Elissa's lips twitched into a slight smirk before she forced on a frown. "Joyce, you're awful," she said with fake disapproval.

Rage surged through Arabella, but she clenched her fists and forced herself to watch to the end.

The girls yanked off the last of Daisy's clothes.She screamed, completely exposed and trembling. Laughing like it was all some twisted game, they poured dirty water over her,ignoring her cries for help.
