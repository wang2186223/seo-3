I was granted entrance. But her realm did not look like it did before.

Everything it was all black, oxidized.

There was no shimmer to the silver grass and trees it was literally tarnished by her tears. The water was murky and dull. The little animals she loved so much were also tarnished, as if they too were tainted by how she felt

I was a villain

An outcast.

The Symbol on my back shoulder and belly button marked me as a coconspirator to make their mistress afraid and upset.

"Both are ancient, high ranking, and stubborn None can stop them, merely question them You're grandfather's first wife, yet this isn't the first universe they've ever made."
