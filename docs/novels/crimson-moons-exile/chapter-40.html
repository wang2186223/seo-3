He was an elk horned weasel-demon in his true form, but I could understand him automatically seeing as how I was meant to be down here permanently eventually.

If I told Willow what animal species he looked like, I'd imagine she'd comment how that sounded cute. But he was not. He was a sulfurous smelling, 12 foot tall, skull-faced demon. "We'll start with your penance. You have 365 days to go. Then I'll train you. Your punishment will have you see all the layers Who knows you might ask the master to stay once we're done." He said summoning a flaming whip. It held barbs on it like rose thorns, while its end held 13 cat tails.
