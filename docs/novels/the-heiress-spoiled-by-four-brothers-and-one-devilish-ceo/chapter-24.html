The second Tristan's icy voice cut through the silence, the five guys visibly cracked.

The bald guy stammered, lips trembling, "P-please, Mr. Reid... I just got a call, said there was a girl, totally free. Told me to wait outside Prestige. Then a van with fake plates pulled up and just tossed the girl-uh, the little girl-right in front of me."

Kevin dialed back.

A robotic voice came through, "Sorry, the numnber you dialed is no longer in service..."

He turned slightly to glance at Tristan. "Disconnected."

The bald guy crumpled, his whole body folding down like jelly. "It was a setup... we're screwed!"

Kevin lit a cigarette, took one calm drag. "You recognize the driver?"

Tears instantly gushed down the guy's face. "N-no... I was too busy staring at the chick... I mean, the little girl!"

Kevin's face darkened. He remembered that tiny girl sitting on the sofa earlier. What kind of sicko could do that to someone so small?

He sneered coldly. "You knew she was just a little girl, and you still did it? On our turf, too. You're really tired of living,huh?"He stood up abruptly and walked straight over.

Kevin'slips curled into a cruel smile, and he brought the tweezers towards the guy's fingernail.

"Mmmphmmph!" the man screamed in muffled agony.

One by one, the nails came off, no hesitation.

The others watched in horror, shaking in their boots, but there was nowhere to run.

The cries echoed through the entire basement as bright red blood soaked into the stone floor beneath five mangled hands.

Tristan stood up, posture casual, and strolled over with his hands in his pockets. "Which one of you tore my wife's dress?"

The other four immediately turned and pointed at him.

Baldy wanted to scream. He was innocent! That woman tore it herself so she could beat them senseless!How was that his fault?!

The gag came off. "She tore it herself!" the bald guy sobbed. "Wrapped the fabric around her hands and used it to beat us!"

Kevin blinked, suddenly getting it. So the skirtwas in the way-Megan ripped it for convenience. Damn,she really was something. Took down five full-grown men like it was nothing. Tristan only snorted. "My wife's shy and soft-hearted. How could she possibly lay a finger on you? You terrified her."

CRACK! CRACK! CRACK!

With that, he turned and strode off.

Kevin threw the glove to the floor, visibly disgusted. He stared at the blood on his hands, his lips pursing slightly. Tristan's words echoed in his mind.

What exactly did they see?

Then it hit him-Megan's bright, bare legs.

A cold sweat broke out on his back. If he weren't Tristan's friend, would he have gotten his eyeballs dug out too?

Shaking off the shivers, he started to leave-only to circle back. He was seriously curious how that woman, Megan, took down all five guys.

Looking to one of the bodyguards, he said, "Take the cloth out of those two guys' mouths."

The bodyguard did as told.

Kevin narrowed his eyes at the two. "What move took you out?"

Tears streamed down their faces.

"Instant Egg Explosion."

"Sad Songs for the Eggs."

Damn. She didn't hold back at all.
