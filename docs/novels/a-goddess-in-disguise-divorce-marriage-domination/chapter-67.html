She decided not to reveal everything just yet. She had already discovered the Jenkins family's real motive for taking her in. For now, she kept the knowledge that they only wanted a living donor under wraps, saving it for a time when it wvould serve her best.

Evelyn, too blinded by pride, missed the warning buried in Alexia's tone. Her voice iced over as fury took the reins. "Mess with Zayne's future and you'll find me standing in your way every step."

With a laugh that held no warmth, Alexia answered, "Isn't it something? Your world starts and ends with your son, and anyone who dares cross you gets an earful. Feels like a never-ending fight, doesn't it? Save some of that spirit for Jenkins Group's plummeting shares. Maybe run the numbers on what your beloved business is about to lose."

Rage made Evelyn's voice tremble, and her next words barely broke free. "You little-!"
