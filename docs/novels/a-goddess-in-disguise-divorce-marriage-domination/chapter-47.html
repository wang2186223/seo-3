Gaining such recognition as a fresh, local brand,though, was an ordeal fit for a Greek tragedy Roadblocks rose at every turn, each more daunting than the last.

Waiting wasn't an option. Jenkins Group needed allies, and in Northbay, only one name held enough weight: Goldrose Consortium.Their network stretched everywhere, and their reputation was unmatched.

Frankly, Goldrose Consortium hadn't lied; Jenkins Group's credentials fell short of their standards. Still,why extend an olive branch if they truly saw the group as beneath them?

Just now, they'd ridiculed Zayne's efforts. Yet not so long ago, the same voices had praised his sharp mind and quick judgment. Had their admiration been genuine, or was it all an act?

Those thoughts bounced around in Zayne's mind until a single name interruptad-Luna

He still had Luna. She was a legend in the finance world, a mysterious presence he'd encountered online by sheer luck. Every crisisthat had threatened to sink him before, Luna had steered him through. Even the connection with Goldrose Consortium could be traced back to her.

Excitement replaced his doubts. Zayne's fingers moved quickly. He tapped into an old chat, searching for the familiar golden star icon, and sent off a message,hoping for a lifeline.

The message bounced back, undelivered.

Staring at the screen, Zayne felt a sharp rush of anxiety.

He had been blacklisted.

His secretary, catching the change in his expression.spoke up quietly. "Is something wrong, sir?"

Not a single word left Zayne's lips for several minutes.Fear held him still, rooting him to the spot.

She was the one who had asked for his friendship.Why was she the first to walk away?

Despite all the admiration and hope he'd placed in her,she vanished without leaving a trace or a reason.

Now that Luna had disappeared, what future did Jenkins Group have?

Confusion swirled in his mind. Luna had always been his guiding star. Now, she was gone, and he felt completely lost.
