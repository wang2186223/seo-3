Eliana, who had once firmly opposed Rodger's decision to adopt Grace, now found herself quietly humbled. That very orphan she despised had just saved her life.

The emotion welled up so suddenly, it left her momentarily speechless. She didn't quite know how to describe it. Gratitude? Regret? Both?

Gianna,ever persistent, tried to downplay Grace's role.She kept insisting that it was Kevin who had pulled Eliana back from the brink of death. But Eliana wasn't naive. She could piece the truth together on her own.

Besides, if it hadn't been for Grace's sharp warning back then, she might never have realized how serious her illness truly was.

The thought made her lips press together in hesitation.After a moment's pause, she finally looked at Grace and murmured,"Thank you."

Just two simple words-but coming from Eliana,who was naturally proud and rarely admitted fault, they carried immense weight.

Gianna,upon hearing this, was stunned-and completely unwilling to accept it. Though her face remained composed, a storm of unease churned inside her.How swiftly had this wretched Grace managed to charm even Eliana? No, she wouldn't allow that.Absolutely not.

With a carefully measured tone, Gianna said, "Aunt Eliana, I've heard of a rare medicine called the Rejuvenation Pill. It's supposed to be very effective for your condition. There's an auction in a few days where it will be available, and Claire and I will do everything we can to get it for you."

Gianna intentionally included Claire in her statement,subtly expressing her willingness while ensuring that Claire would be the one footing the bill.

Claire, oblivious to her scheming, quickly declared,"Yes! I've heard those pills are amazing for recovering from serious illnesses." If they could save her mother,she'd stop at nothing to get them.

"Indeed, the Rejuvenation Pill is excellent for post-surgical recovery and can even help prevent the spread of tumor cells. Three pills eliminate the need for chemotherapy," Grace assured, a bit surprised that Gianna and Claire were aware of the drug.

"That's wonderful news," Eliana responded, her will to survive intensifying. At the thought of escaping the

torment of chemotherapy, her heart swelled with hope and gratitude toward Grace.

Everyone knew how brutal chemotherapy could be-the side effects alone were enough to break a person.The idea of bypassing that pain lit a fire of determination in Eliana's heart.

If the pill could truly spare her such suffering, then no price would be too high.

"Perfect! Mom, I swear I'll get the Rejuvenation Pill for you on the day of the auction!" Claire's eyes gleamed with resolve.

But that glimmer of hope didn't last long.

"The Rejuvenation Pill isn't easy to come by," Kevin interjected flatly. "It's a rare treasure and highly sought after.
